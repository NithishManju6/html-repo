Benefits

Referral Bonus Program

Refer candidates who meet the qualifications to Human Resources using the Candidate Referral Form. If your candidate is hired you will be awarded $500 within 180 days of their hire date.

Employee Assistance Program

Confidential help is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week to all Extendime employees and their families. Available assistance includes counseling for personal or job-related concerns, substance abuse, legal or financial advice, and referrals for child or elder care.

Educational Assistance

Employees who have completed 180 days of employment may be eligible for financial assistance for classes directly related to their position.

Vacation/PTO

Vacation/Personal Paid Time Off, beginning immediately. Unused accrued time may be carried over from one calendar year to the next.

Medical Benefits

Extendime provides a Group Medical Coverage for all of its employees and their families through a major Insurance provider.

Quarterly Bonus

Extendime has taken an initiative to provide quarterly bonus based on the position and performance to all it's employees who have completed 180 of employment.
